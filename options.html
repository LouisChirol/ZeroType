Usage Instructions

1
Focus on any text field

Click on any input field, textarea, or editable content where you want to insert text.

2
Start recording

Press Ctrl+Space (or Cmd+Space on Mac) to start recording your voice.

3
Speak clearly

Speak your text clearly. The recording overlay will show while you're recording.

4
Stop and transcribe

Press the shortcut again or click "Stop Recording" to transcribe and insert the text.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Ctrl+Space Start/Stop recording (Windows/Linux)
Cmd+Space Start/Stop recording (Mac)
